The Python for Data Science Curriculum, in collaboration with PSU, aims at introducing students to the general scientific computing challenges posed by cutting edge research in domains such as: engineering, computer science, economics, business intelligence, nanotecnology, and industrial connectivity and security.

OVERVIEW

The training program “Python for Data Science” is designed to provide a comprehensive introduction for either beginners with basic programming background or more advanced developers who would like to step into the world of data science, scientific computing and analytics. At the end of this training course, you will have the required skills to go deeper into more technical data science areas and you will have the necessary background to study hot topics like deep learning and neural networks, and data analytics.

PREREQUISITES

Anyone with some basic programming background can benefit from the training. Preliminary knowledge of Python is recommended but not necessary. A Crash course on Python will be provided to present the essential concepts. The training program will present all the essential material to access, store, manipulate, visualize, process and analyze data using Python. Target Audience might inlcude students or instructors willing to start their data science learning path, beginner/advanced developers with an interest in data science, and employees in government, private, and industrial sector with an IT background.
